Search the Archive.

This page allows you to search the archive for specific events. There are three datasources:

  • The live stream captured in realtime each night. Available only for 30 days. Clicking on the image will show a larger version in a new window.
  • Matched events, for which we've solved to determine the orbit and trajectory. Clicking on the image will take you to the detailed report page, where there is also a link to download the raw data
  • Single detections, not matched with any other data in our archive. Clicking on the image will allow you to download the image.
    • A detection may appear in all three datasets. Note however that many of our cameras upload only upload analytical information on a monthly basis, so single and matched analysis is often not available for some days or weeks.

    The data here are released under the CC BY 4.0 license, so if you are using the data whether for scientific or other purposes, your must reference this web site in your work.
