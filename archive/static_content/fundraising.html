Fundraising

Our Justgiving is now closed for 2023.

However, if you'd still like to help with data analysis costs, you can donate directly via our bank account. This has the advantage that we are not charged administration fees by JustGiving, and also we are not taxed on the donations. As we're not a charity, Justgiving donations are treated as earnings of the person who sets up the fundraiser and are subject to tax at their marginal rate. However, funds paid directly into our account can be offset against costs with only the balance being taxed, if above the tax threshold.

If you're interested in donating via our bank account please contact markmcintyre99 at googlemail.com for further details.
